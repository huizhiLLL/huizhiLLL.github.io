Overview

About speedcubing and our community

What is Speedcubing?

Speedcubing is the activity of solving a variety of twisty puzzles, the most famous being the Rubik's Cube, as quickly as possible. Participants compete in various events with different puzzle types and solving methods.

Our Community

We are a group of speedcubing enthusiasts who meet regularly to practice, compete, and share techniques. Our community welcomes cubers of all skill levels, from beginners to speedcubing experts.

Events

Competition and practice events

Weekly Practice

Join us every Saturday at 3 PM for practice sessions. We help beginners learn to solve the cube and experienced cubers improve their times.

Monthly Competition

We host official competitions monthly with WCA events including 3x3, 2x2, 4x4, and specialty events like blindfolded solving.

Tournaments

We organize regional tournaments twice a year, attracting cubers from across the area to compete for prizes and recognition.
